About

There is no one size fits all when it comes to a fitness regime. People come in many different shapes and sizes so, inevitably, so should their training programmes.

Compleat Fitness personal training and nutrition packages are tailored specifically to your needs, goals, and preferences, so that you can get exactly what you want out of your fitness and diet regime. All packages include a one to one health and fitness consultation, diet analysis, personalised goal setting and text and email support throughout the programme, so whether your goal is fat loss, muscle gain, improved general fitness or anything in between, I'll design an achievable programme that works for you.
