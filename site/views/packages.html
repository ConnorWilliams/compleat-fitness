FAQs

Are there any alternatives to these packages?
All of the above packages are flexible and can be tailored to suit your needs. One-off sessions are also available at a cost of £32 per session.
What will a personal training session involve?
A 45-60 minute workout with one-to-one instruction and motivation throughout. Each session will be specifically designed for you, according to your excercise preferences and your fitness and health goals.
What is a diet analysis?
At the start of your programme, you will be given the opportunity to complete a 3 day food diary for the purpose of analysis and guidance. I will provide constructive nutrition advice based on the food diary you provide, helping you to make adjustments to your diet to get the most out of your training programme.
